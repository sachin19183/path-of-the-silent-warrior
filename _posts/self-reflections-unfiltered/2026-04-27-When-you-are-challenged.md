There was a phase in silent warrior's life when he was not silent at all :P . He was in a mode of giving it back or one can say that his reply would be in the same language/tone/intention in which the question was asked. That were his youthful days when he was full of confidence and energy. That he would conquer the world. In his late 30's he became subdued, gave in to the corporate toxicity and became a silent warrior. Doing his work in silence, not giving much after thought to the whirlwind politics around him.
 But of late , once he has build his website, a new era of confidence has started to seep in again. And he is now back in the mood of giving it back.
 some non negotiables for him are:
 - Questioning his integrity
 - Questioning his professional ethics
 - Direct interference in his work

Sometimes, the world around you only understands tough love. You need to be mature enough to understand when the time is right to giv e this tough love.
For the silent warrior, the time has arrived. So he is now going in full war prep mode to upskill in quick time and then give it back via his marvellous show off of his end products- which will be a coding assistant which can not only code, but can do code review, integrate with a CI pipeline and implement gate checks for code commits.

Wish him all the best!!!!
