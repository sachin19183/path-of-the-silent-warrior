This post is a collection of inspiring and Food For Thought kind of statements/quotes that i have come across.

- You are not competing with anybody else but yourself
- At the end of the day, ask yourself, did you learn anything new today? did you improved by an iota today?
- Are you a missionary or a mercenary? If you are a missionary, then just build whatever you want to build that really speaks to your heart.
  But if you are a mercenary, then you want to really figure out are you trying to get for that internship? Are you trying to get that specific prize?
  In that case, who do you want to kind of communicate with and have that conversation with? -Chinat Yu (Founder of MentorMates)
- What a strange gift it is to hit rock bottom. To be stripped of pride, ego, and the illusions you once holded on to so badly.
  To stand at zero and yet see possibilities everywhere. Foe when a man has nothing left to loose, he finally learns that he is free to build anything.
  The man who has fallen and rebuilt himself knows no fear, no doubt and no limits. He is in every sense alive
